IOB CSV Format Explanation

IOB CSV format for BERT NER training data with columns:

  • words: The token/word
  • sentence_id: Sentence number (0, 1, 2...)
  • labels: IOB tag (O, B-Disease, I-Disease, etc.)

Tag meanings:

  • B-TYPE: Beginning of an entity
  • I-TYPE: Inside (continuation) of an entity
  • O: Outside any entity (regular word)

Example:

words,sentence_id,labels
The,0,O
patient,0,O
has,0,O
diabetes,0,B-Disease
mellitus,0,I-Disease
.,0,O
Treatment,1,O
includes,1,O
insulin,1,B-Substance
